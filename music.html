The B-52's Discography

Forty years and twenty million albums into their career, the B-52's remain one of the most beloved and enduring bands. From groundbreaking songs like “Rock Lobster,” “Dance This Mess Around” and “Private Idaho“ to chart-topping hits like “Love Shack” and “Roam” and “Deadbeat Club”, their unforgettable tunes start a dance party every time the music begins.
